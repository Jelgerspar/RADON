20-phase plan explained.


What is the 20-phase plan?
In this plan we try to improve the coin’s price by increasing the price per phase. We bring in each phase more coins in circulation with a higher price per coin.
After each drop of coins, we wait 1 month for another drop to let the coin stabilize, by letting people trade with the coin.

What is the point of the 20-phase plan?
With this plan we try to artificially increase the price. The buyer can make money by buying the coin in an early phase and sell it in a later phase. After all the phases Radon will be a trading tool used for day trading or holding the coin and hope the price will increase over time.
